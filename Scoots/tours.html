TOURS

Hola! You've arrived at Scoots Tours, the best activities and tours in Cozumel Mexico. Come on in and have a look at all the amazing things to do in Cozumel! Discounts on every tour and huge savings on cruise shore excursions too!.

Tour from Cozumel to the Mayan Ruins Of Tulum

Do not miss out on Cozumel's famous Mayan Ruins! Learn about Mayan civilization, culture, folklore, and unsolved mysteries. We are specialists in the Mayan culture and would love to share our passion for this civilization by taking you to the most reputed Mayan sites in the Mexican State of Quintana Roo.

Cozumel Horseback Riding

We offer great options for Horseback Riding in Cozumel Mexico. Explore Mayan Ruins and Ancient Caves, then head for the beach where you can relax at an exclusive beach resort for the rest of the day. Our horseback rides are suitable for kids and families of all ages, which makes this a very fun family activity.

COZUMEL ATV Raod Tour

There is no better way to explore the dense jungles and beautiful but deserted beaches of Cozumel's East shore than on an ATV tour. The Eastern half of Cozumel is virtually uninhabited, and seldom visited. There is only one road, and that ends before you reach your destination! You'll board your ATV and ride through the wilds of Cozumel island, visiting Mayan Villages, snorkeling on remote coral reefs, and even seeing ancient Mayan Ruins and caves!.

Swim With Dolphins in Playa Del Carmen

Get into the water at one of the largest dolphin parks along the Riviera Maya for a chance to meet and play with the creatures by the beautiful shores of Playa Del Carmen. You will get one-on-one time and plenty of fun with some of the cleverest creatures in the sea.
