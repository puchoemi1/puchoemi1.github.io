Target Audience

▶ Site destinated to anyone interested in weather conditions. By Default they will see the live weather forecast in the area they live.

▶ They will be able to access the extended forecast to see the impact of weather in wathever place they will go in the comming days. ▶ Visitors will be able to access the site from desktops computers, tablets and mobile devices.

Personas

▶ Henry (32) is a fisher man who loves to go out for experience nature over the lakes and mountains. He will daily check weather conditions on his smartphone, specially the wind section to see what fly fishing technique he will apply during the adventure.

▶ Sofia (40) is an accountant, she loves to go to the office on her bicycle every day and of course she access the site checkoutweather.com from her smartphone that will tell her to take some recommendations for the live weather conditions she will go through every day.

Scenarios

▶ How will be the weather today? I need to laundry.

▶ On Saturday we expect to go out for hiking. Should we check out the weather forecast on the internet?

▶ I want to bike to work tomorrow. How will the weather be tomorrow?

▶ We are planning vacations for this weekend. Should check the weather to see what activities we do?
