Alignment

This website has applied the principle of allignment in a very organized way that helps the customer to relax and scan the page with anxiety. Elements seem to be perfectly alligned and decorative.

Contrast

The contrast between the text, text backgroud and the banner picture makes a really good combination of colors that impact. The designer creates focal point for an audience and a visual hierarchy.

Balance

The designer did a really good example of balance principle with the banner picture. It seems the expectator is flying up into the air. The visual weight is distributed.
